Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information (name, email) from your social login provider
  • Child information provided by parents/guardians (name, age, grade level)
  • Usage data and performance metrics on worksheets
  • Technical information about your device and browser

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and personalize our educational services
  • Generate age-appropriate math worksheets
  • Track progress and adjust difficulty levels
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Communicate with you about your account

3. Children's Privacy

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We only collect children's personal information with parental consent and use it solely for educational purposes. Parents can review, update, or delete their children's information at any time.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Information is stored in secure databases with encryption at rest and in transit. We regularly review and update our security practices.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Social login providers (Google, Facebook, X) for authentication
  • Amazon Web Services for data storage
  • Analytics tools to improve our service

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Export your data

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or through the service.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at privacy@mathtutor.com.
